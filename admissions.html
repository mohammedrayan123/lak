Admissions Process

We are thrilled that you are considering Little Angels Kindergarten for your child's early education. Discover our simple steps to becoming a part of our Little Angels family.

1

Schedule a Tour

Come see our magical garden in person! Explore the classrooms and meet our loving educators.

2

Submit Application

Pick up and submit the application form in person at the school office. (Note: Applications must be submitted in person, not online).

3

Playdate Visit

Your child joins us for a short play session to ensure our environment is the perfect fit.

4

Welcome Home!

Once enrolled, you'll receive a welcome packet with all the details for their first day!
