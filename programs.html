Our Educational Programs

We offer a variety of programs tailored to your child's developmental stage. Each program is designed to inspire creativity, independence, and a lifelong love of learning.

* All ages are as on 1st July

2 - 2.5 Years

Play-Group

Our Play-Group focuses on sensory exploration, language development, and early socialization in a safe, loving environment.

  • Focus: Sensory play, motor skills, basic vocabulary
Enroll Now
2.5 - 3.5 Years

Nursery

The Nursery program builds on curiosity through play-based learning, focusing on cognitive development, early literacy, and peer interaction.

  • Focus: Storytelling, creative arts, early numbers
Enroll Now
3.5 - 4.5 Years

Junior KG (L. KG)

Our Junior KG program introduces structured learning while maintaining a fun atmosphere. We emphasize foundational reading, writing, and logical thinking.

  • Focus: Phonics, numeracy, project-based learning
Enroll Now
4.5 - 5.5 Years

Senior KG (U. KG)

The final step before primary school. Senior KG focuses on school readiness, advanced communication skills, and independent problem-solving.

  • Focus: Reading fluency, basic math operations, environmental awareness
Enroll Now
