Our Story

Growing with Love.

Little Angels Kindergarten comprises of Play School, Nursery, Junior Kg and Senior Kg with a single division each. It's a place for the kids to learn, play and basically have a good time which is the need of early childhood.

Our Philosophy

Nature inspired learning spaces

A preschool is a child’s first step into the world of learning, and at LAK, we strive to make that journey meaningful, joyful, and inspiring. Nestled in a safe, hygienic, and peaceful environment with smart classrooms and expansive green surroundings, we provide a nurturing space where young minds can explore, discover, and grow.

Our vision is to create a stimulating and supportive atmosphere that fosters the holistic development of every child—kindling curiosity, strengthening character, and nurturing a lifelong love for learning. Through a blend of academic excellence and experiential learning opportunities, we encourage children to think creatively, build confidence, and develop compassion. At LAK, we believe that when learning is fun and engaging, children become active participants in their own growth. Our mission is to empower young learners with the knowledge, values, and skills they need to become confident, creative, and responsible citizens of tomorrow.

Traditional School

  • Teacher - Centered
  • Learning through structured academics
  • Focus on Memorization
  • Group Instruction
  • Discipline is externally controlled

Our Montessori Based School

  • Child – Centered
  • Learning through curiosity, creativity and practical life skills
  • Focus on child – led exploration and pace
  • Respect for each child’s individuality
  • Self Discipline is encouraged naturally
