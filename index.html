About Us

Little Angels is more than a preschool—it is a caring learning community where children are encouraged to explore, discover, and thrive. Through a balanced blend of play, creativity, and structured learning, we help young minds build confidence, independence, and essential life skills.

Our Philosophy

Nature inspired learning spaces

Traditional School

  • Teacher - Centered
  • Learning through structured academics
  • Focus on Memorization
  • Group Instruction
  • Discipline is externally controlled

Our Montessori Based School

  • Child – Centered
  • Learning through curiosity, creativity and practical life skills
  • Focus on child – led exploration and pace
  • Respect for each child’s individuality
  • Self Discipline is encouraged naturally
